### Exercise 7.02
Which of the following are not legal constants in C? Classify each legal constant as either integer or floating-point.  
(a) `010E2`  
(b) `32.1845E+5`  
(c) `0790`  
(d) `100_000`  
(e) `3.978e-2`  

### Solution
(c) (d) are not legal constants.

(a) (b) (e) are all legal floating-point constants.
